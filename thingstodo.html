Immerse Yourself in the Vibrant of each Festivals

Let the wonders of Myanmar's festivals captivate your senses and create unforgettable memories. Embrace the vibrant energy, immerse yourself in the rich cultural traditions, and discover the hidden treasures of each city.

April 13 to April 16 each year

Thingyan Water Festival

4 Days
Yangon and Malanday

Thingyan, Myanmar's Water Festival, is a lively celebration marking the Burmese New Year. Lasting for several days in mid-April, it involves joyful water-throwing festivities symbolizing cleansing and renewal.

November 16th to November 22nd

Taunggyi Balloon Festival

7 Days
Shan State

The Taunggyi Balloon Festival in Myanmar is a vibrant event in Taunggyi, featuring a mesmerizing display of hot air balloons. During the nighttime competitions, beautifully designed balloons adorned with candles and fireworks light up the sky.

The festival vary each year based on the lunar calendar.
